                       Application Instructions:
10 random questions based on the knowledge of irregular English verbs will be displayed on the screen in turn.
The answer should be entered only in lower case in English, there should not be extra characters and spaces.
After entering the answer, you must press the "Enter" key to continue the test.
Next, the program will display the correct answer and the number of correct user's answers at the moment.
At the end, the user can enter his name to save the result in a text file. If the user wants to exit to the main menu, he needs to enter the line "exit".
