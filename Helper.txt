Instructions for use:
The user enters the number of questions that will be answered. They are based on knowledge of irregular English verbs and will be displayed on the screen in turn.
There should not be extra characters and spaces. To continue the test, press the "Enter" key.
At the end, the user can enter his name to save the result in a text list.
If the user wants to exit to the main menu, he needs to enter the line “exit”.
